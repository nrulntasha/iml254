My Background

I will once more announce myself as Nurul Natasha Bt Abdul Rahim. I go by Shasha with my pals. I was born in Kuantan's St. Chong Clinic on June 28, 2004. All five of my siblings are girls. I'm the middle child. They said milddle child suffer the most. To be honest, I don't dispute it. I used to believe that because of our age difference, my sisters despise me. My second sister was three years older than my first, who was six years older. We were living at different levels, and the age difference alone made me uneasy. As time passes, I discover that they were a little uncomfortable around me because we didn't have much time together because they were attending boarding school. My mother passed away in 2019, yet we were reunited in the end. After being married, my first sister had a really cute daughter named Hanna Mikayla. She had me totally enamoured!
