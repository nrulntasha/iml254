SMK Seri Pekan, 26600 Pekan, Pahang Three years in SMK Seri Pekan was simply priceless to me. My heart has particular place for it. Back then, I was clueless and couldn't have given enough attention. Though my friends are encouraging, I am average academically. They always support me when I learn a little sluggish. As it seems, boarding school is not simple. I have to disicpline myself to get up early, goes to school, returns to similar schedule. I used to sneak a phone back then. During my stay, there are plenty of bittersweet memories.
SM Teknik Kuantan, Lorong Alor Akar 76, Taman Aman, 25250 Kuantan, Pahang For those who do not know, Technical Institute Kuantan is just for upper form, which includes grades 4 and 5. A number of technical courses are offered, including accounting, engineering, mechanical, and electrical. For SPM, ten subjects must be taken. I found it difficult at first. That's when I took accounting. Among other things, we have a ton of extra lessons and endless assignments. Sincerely, it was hard, but I made it through high school. Back in Form 5, I was chosen to be MPP. I was a member of the team that edited our school magazine. I could always count on my roommates and classmates to support me. They are such a blessing to me.
UiTM Cawangan Kedah, 08400 Merbok, Kedah In March 2023, I began working towards my diploma in library informatics. Since it was my first time being away from home, it was really difficult for me. While it is true that I attended boarding school from 2017 to 2021, I have never lived so far away from home. My goal is to earn a first-class diploma, therefore I prepare myself to remain optimistic. I am constantly learning new things that help me become a better person. I've known my classmates since the first semester, and they never fail to support me when I need it. I am really grateful to them for supporting me through everything.
